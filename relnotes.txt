eskil22.kit is a platform independent starkit, executable
by tclkit or by tclsh in many Tcl installations.

Standalone executables for a few platform are available
consisting of starpacks (tclkit+starkit wrapped in one).

Any of the files can be unwrapped by sdx to get the source.

All versions need access to a "diff" executable.
